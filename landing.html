Questions

Everything you're wondering

Is it really free? +

Yes. You can use Task OS free, starting with no account at all. It's an independent product built by one maker — no venture pressure to nickel-and-dime you. If paid plans ever arrive, the core of what you use today stays free.

Do I have to sign up to try it? +

No. Click “Start now” and you're in immediately as a guest, with a starter board ready to go. Create a free account later — in one tap — only when you want to sync across devices. Your guest work carries over.

Who can see my tasks? +

Only you. Each account's data is isolated at the database level with row-level security, and offline copies live on your own device. There are no ads and your data is never sold.

Does it work on my phone? +

Yes — it's a progressive web app that installs to your home screen on iPhone and Android and runs full-screen like a native app. It works on desktop the same way, and syncs between them.

What does the AI actually do — and is it safe? +

The AI triages your inbox into priorities and drafts a time-blocked day on request. It only acts when you ask it to, and you approve the result. It's an assistant, not an autopilot.

Can I get my data out if I leave? +

Always. Export your tasks and goals to a file whenever you like. No lock-in — if Task OS isn't for you, you leave with everything.
